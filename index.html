Is this book good?

Don’t read another “bad” book again. We built a Chrome extension to help readers avoid wasting time and regret bad book purchases by evaluating books on 3 criteria: 1. how recently did the book come out? 2. what's the buyer success rate for people who bought the book? 3. what percentage of people have rated the book 1-star? We then judge these scores on a pass/fail basis and show these scores only on Amazon book's product pages so you are instantly informed.
